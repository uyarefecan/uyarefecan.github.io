Hello!

In 2013, coming first as a result of the talent exam, I won the right to study at Dokuz Eylül University Faculty of Fine Arts in Izmir, Turkey. I graduated from Film Design and Directing Department in 2017.During my education I written, directed and edited 9 short films. Among these films, "The Receipt", "13 Hours" and "4 Walls" are held in various festivals and competitions; has received screenings and / or awards.

In addition to Adobe software such as Premiere Pro, After Effects, Photoshop and Illustrator, which I have gained a lot of experience in during my studies, I've also developed and continue to develop myself in software such as NukeX and Blender 3D. I did two paid internships, one as a set supervisor during the pre-production of the feature film "Wish to Wash With Rain" and other as a videographer and video editor at Tiramisu Productions.

In 2007, I started working as a freelancer for video production, photography, menu, packaging, graphic design, social media management and web designer. I co-established Design Corp. #9 - Visual Communications and Archtitecture firm in late 2017 and designed and directed various projects for many firms and customers in fields like music, health, construction, food and beauty.

In this page, you can find information about my projects, experiences, software I use and languages I speak. You can also find viewing links for all my films.

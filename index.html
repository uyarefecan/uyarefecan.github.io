Hello There!

My name is Efe Can, let me introduce myself.

I have always been passionate about problem-solving, storytelling, and simplifying processes for those around me. After my formal education in filmmaking, the problem-solving aspect of my personality led me to explore the UX field. I find great satisfaction in delving to the root of a problem, developing stories around it, and devising practical solutions. I believe that the ideal means to actualize my passion is through UX design.

From 2018 to late 2022, I worked solely as a digital content creator. During this time, I created animations, produced films, captured photographs, created and customized websites for clients.

After engaging in extensive study sessions, I have successfully completed the Google UX Design Professional Certificate on Coursera. As a next step in my career in UX design, I have created a website to showcase my portfolio. This platform will enable me to present my skills, knowledge, and experience in the field of UX design and thereby, advance my career prospects.

Thank you so much for your interest in my work! I am thrilled to have the opportunity to share my portfolio with you. I can't wait to hear your thoughts!
